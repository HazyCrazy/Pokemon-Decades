Decades is not designed for Nuzlockes or Challenge Runs.
There is nothing stopping you from giving it a go.

But outside of the PSS (Pokemon Storage System) Suite, and the Trainer Suite.
Decades will never be a Radical Red, Elite Redux or Run and Bun Pokemon fan game.

Decades has no native support for Randomizers, and the Random Starters Option
is the closest you will get to a Randomizer.

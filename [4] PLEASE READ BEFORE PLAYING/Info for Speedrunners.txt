Decades uses plugins such as End of Round Skipper, EV Allocator,
and general under the hood changes in code, to remove 
as much downtime in the tedium of battles and menus as possible.

Your Trainer Suite the second you click New Game comes with 
the Fast Travel Phone Key Item (PAS, Phone Assisted Speedrun)

It can instantly and optimally fast travel you to anywhere in the game,
and can toggle Damage Rolls to be 100%.

It is possible with a PAS Speedrun, to beat the game in under 5 minutes or less.

By using F12 (Soft Reset) after saving the game, navigation through specfic
menus or animations will become faster. 

Please set the Refresh Rate of your monitor to 60hrz for Decades,
for the correct framerate timings. Or set to higher, for faster times.

In the Pokemon Party Menu, you can click the [Z] Key to quick Swap a Pokemon's order.
In the overworld, when using the Singles Battle Format, you can press [S]
to quick swap the first Pokemon sent out.
